Booking Tips

Book 6-12 Months Ahead

This isn't an exaggeration. Good group properties for Mardi Gras book up a year in advance. If it's October and you haven't booked, you're already late.

Expect Higher Prices

Mardi Gras week commands premium rates everywhere. Budget 2-3x normal prices. It's worth it, but plan accordingly.

Minimum Stays Apply

Most properties require 4-7 night minimums during Mardi Gras. Plan to stay the full stretch—you'll want to anyway.
